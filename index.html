Mr. Sakthivel is the main reason to start our All india kalam foundation and he supported and guided us to help people through the foundation . He completed Bachelor of Engineering. He put has all efforts and also maximum earnings to help people through All india kalam foundation . He was the role model for youngsters .He always believes in the line of “Doing nothing for others is the undoing of ourselves”.

The organisation came into existence with the hard work of like-minded individuals headed by Mr. Sakthivel .who had the passion to render services to the underprivileged, oppressed, women, children and differently abled in the most backward areas of Omalur Taluk, Salem District. It is a charitable, non-profit, non-political, non-communal organization, committed to the objectives of All India kalam foundation
